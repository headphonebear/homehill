## üõ†Ô∏è Open Source Projects

### Core Infrastructure

**Alpine Linux**  
Minimalist, fast, secure. The perfect foundation for "Naked OS Brutalism."

**Docker & Docker Compose**  
Containerization made elegant. Services that know their place.

**Kubernetes (K3s)**  
Lightweight orchestration for the Orchard cluster. Power without bloat.

**Traefik**  
Reverse proxy with soul. Automatic TLS, beautiful routing, GitOps-friendly.

### Databases & Storage

**PostgreSQL**  
The world's most advanced open source database. Reliable, powerful, respectful.

**DragonflyDB**  
25x faster than Redis. Modern in-memory data store for mk3's performance.

**Btrfs**  
Copy-on-write filesystem with snapshots. "Never lose data" philosophy in action.

### Services & Applications

**Jellyfin**  
Media server that respects your freedom. No telemetry, no tracking, just music.

**Navidrome**  
Subsonic-compatible music server. Elegant, fast, perfect for mobile apps.

**Memos**  
Note-taking with privacy. Simple, effective, self-hosted.

**Portainer**  
Docker management UI. Makes container oversight accessible to everyone.
